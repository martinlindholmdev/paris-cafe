About Us

A corner of calm in St. Pauli

GrauPi started as a simple idea: good coffee, honest food, and a place to slow down. We opened our doors in 2018 and have been part of the neighborhood ever since.

Our space is filled with mismatched chairs, warm light, and the sound of good conversation. Whether you're here for breakfast, a late lunch, or just a quiet moment — you're welcome to stay.
